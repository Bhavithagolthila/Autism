Child Behavior Questionnaire

1. Does your child have difficulty maintaining eye contact during interactions?

2. Does your child show interest in activities involving social interaction with others?

3. Does your child prefer to be alone rather than engage with others?

4. Does your child exhibit repetitive behaviors, such as hand flapping or rocking?

5. Does your child have trouble understanding or interpreting social cues?

6. Does your child prefer routines and get upset when routines are disrupted?

7. Does your child have difficulty understanding emotions, either their own or others'?

8. Does your child have a narrow range of interests or focus intensely on one activity?

9. Does your child have difficulty communicating needs or desires, either verbally or non-verbally?

10. Does your child show a preference for repetitive behaviors or routines (such as watching the same video repeatedly)?
